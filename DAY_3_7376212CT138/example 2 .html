COIMBATORE

Coimbatore is one of the major metropolitan cities in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu
It is located on the banks of the Noyyal River and surrounded by the Western Ghats
Coimbatore is the second largest city in Tamil Nadu after Chennai and the 16th largest urban agglomeration in India as per the census 2011.
The colorful and intricately carved Arulmigu Patteeswarar Swamy Temple lies southeast of here. In the center, the Gass Forest Museum has a huge collection of preserved animals and tree trunks.

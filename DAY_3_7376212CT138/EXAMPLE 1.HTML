COIMBATORE

is one of the major metropolitan cities in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu
It is located on the banks of the Noyyal River and surrounded by the Western Ghats
Coimbatore is the second largest city in Tamil Nadu after Chennai and the 16th largest urban agglomeration in India as per the census 2011.

MADURAI

Madurai is one of the longest living cities in the world and a major city in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
It is the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu and the administrative headquarters of Madurai District.
As of the 2011 census, it was the third largest Urban agglomeration in Tamil Nadu after Chennai and Coimbatore and the 44th most populated city in India.

CHENNAI

Chennai also known as Madras[A] (the official name until 1996), is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
The state's largest city in area and population as well, Chennai is located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal.
It is the most prominent cultural, economic and educational centre of South India.
